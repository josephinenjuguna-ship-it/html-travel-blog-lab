My Trip to Fire Island

Fire Island is a beautiful barrier island located on the southern tip of Long Island, NY. While I was there, I visited the historic Fire Island Lighthouse, walked the beaches, and hiked through a rare maritime forest. The island is a tranquil getaway from the city and overflowing with wildlife, quaint boardwalk towns and stunning views of the ocean.

Some of the best aspects of the trip were the sunset from the lighthouse deck, the opportunity to see white-tailed deer running free amongst the trails and swimming in the pristine Atlantic waters. Fire Island really is indeed one of the most unusual natural wonders found in the Northeast
